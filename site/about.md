# about

john trinh is an artist-technologist building infrastructure for creative concepts.

## what i do

junior at NYU's Clive Davis Institute of Recorded Music studying music production, creative marketing, and music technology. recently returned from study abroad in berlin (december 2025).

i make music as [oddhorse](https://odd.horse) — a genre-bending electronic/hyperpop project released through Too Lost label. beyond my own work, i provide professional mixing and mastering services, and build custom performance technology for other artists.

## how i work

when a creative concept requires specific tools or systems that don't exist, i build them. custom MIDI controllers programmed in C++. Max for Live plugins for production workflows. hand-coded websites. TouchDesigner visual systems. the technical implementation serves the creative vision — never the other way around.

if i don't know how to do something today, i will by next week.

## technical background

**music production & audio:** 5+ years in ableton live (primary focus: granular synthesis, spatial audio, vocal processing). proficient in logic pro, pro tools, max/msp. active client work for mixing, mastering, and sound design.

**software development:** max for live plugin development, custom midi controller design (esp32 microcontroller programming), web development (html/css/javascript), python scripting, git version control, touchdesigner for live visuals.

**design & visual:** 3000+ documented hours in blender. adobe creative suite proficiency. brand identity systems, typography, video content creation.

**other tools:** various audio plugins, premiere pro, after effects, davinci resolve, obs studio, notion, chartmetric, google analytics.
