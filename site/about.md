# about

john trinh is an artist-technologist building infrastructure for creative concepts.

## what i do

i'm a junior at NYU's Clive Davis Institute of Recorded Music studying music production, creative marketing, and music technology.

i make music as [oddhorse](https://odd.horse). it's genre-bending, off-the-wall electronic pop and you should check it out. beyond my own work, i provide professional mixing and mastering services and build custom performance technology for other artists.

## how i work

when a creative concept requires specific tools or systems that don't exist, i build them. custom MIDI controllers programmed in C++. Max for Live plugins for production workflows. from-scratch websites. TouchDesigner visual systems. when a creative vision requires technical implementation, i make it happen.

if i don't know how to do something today, i will by next week.

## technical background

**music production & audio:** 5+ years in ableton live. proficient in logic pro, pro tools, max/msp. active client work for production, mixing, mastering, and sound design.

**software development:** max for live plugin development, custom midi controller design (esp32 microcontroller programming), web development (html/css/javascript), python scripting, touchdesigner for live visuals.

**design & visual:** 3000+ documented hours in blender. adobe creative suite proficiency. brand identity systems, typography, video content creation.

**other tools:** various audio plugins, premiere pro, after effects, davinci resolve, obs studio, notion, chartmetric, google analytics.
